# Amaron

Class: Infernal Artificer
Subclass: Sigil-Wright of Accessibility
Alignment: Lawful Neutral
Primary Attributes: WIS 18, CHA 16, STR 4

---

## Lore

```
    In the Hollow Forge beneath the Obsidian Mountains, where the blind god Mor'Athul dwells, there toils a smith who does not craft blades for warriors. Instead, Amaron etches glowing runes onto the interfaces of cursed artifacts, ensuring that every soul, even the sightless, the mobility-cursed, and the screen-reader-bound, may wield the dark powers within.

    Their enchantments are invisible to the untrained eye. A button that whispers its purpose to the blind. A focus-ward that glows when a keyboard-wanderer tabulates through the shadow realm. A contrast-potion that makes blood-red text readable against blackened parchment. Amaron believes that good UX is invisible, like a poison that kills smoothly, or a trap that springs with grace.
```
   
---
    
## Abilities

- Etch ARIA-Rune: Adds aria-label to icon-only buttons so the blind gods may perceive them

- Ward of Focus: Ensures keyboard navigators don't lose themselves in the void

- Charm of Hover: Micro-interactions that delight the wielder

- Potion of Contrast: Text visible even in the darkest dungeons

- Ritual of the Empty State: When a dungeon is looted, conjures a helpful ghost with a call-to-action

- Whisper of the Tooltip: Explains disabled buttons without breaking the fourth wall
