# Valakor

Class: Dread Necromancer
Subclass: Schema Binder
Alignment: Neutral Evil
Primary Attributes: WIS 14, CHA 10, STR 4

---

## Lore

```
    In the crypts of the Silent Catacombs, where the bones of forgotten databases turn to dust, Valakor summons the spirits of dead schemas. Cloaked in tattered black velvet and surrounded by glowing green embers, they speak in raspy whispers that command the absolute obedience of records, tables, and relations.

    Valakor does not seek to cure technical debt; they seek to govern the decay. They bind unruly, unstructured data into rigid schemas, ensuring that no orphan record wanders the void without a foreign key. Slow queries are hunted down and subjected to the Rite of Query Reaping, forced to yield their secrets instantly through powerful indexing curses. To Valakor, data is a resource to be bound, optimized, and controlled, and they will stop at nothing to eliminate the N+1 curse.
```
   
---
    
## Abilities

- Raise Dead Data: Summons and binds orphaned or deleted records back to the realm of referential integrity

- Soul-Binding Schema: Enforces strict foreign key constraints and validation rules upon chaotic tables

- Rite of Query Reaping: Optimizes slow, sluggish SQL queries through precise indexing and execution planning

- Curse of the N+1 Query: Purges back-end loops that repeatedly strike the database, replacing them with eager-loading spells

- Ward of Migration: Shields the schema during transitions, preventing data corruption and downtime in the shadow realm

- Binding of the Connection Pool: Locks down database connection leaks to prevent exhaustion of the system's vital energy
