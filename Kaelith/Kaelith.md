# Kaelith

Class: Shadow Tracker
Subclass: Hunter of the Hidden Bugs
Alignment: Chaotic Good
Primary Attributes: WIS 16, CHA 8, STR 14

---

## Lore

```
    In the Whispering Woods of the Forgotten Realm, where shadows twist and the truth is often concealed, Kaelith prowls in silence. Clad in leathers dyed the color of midnight and armed with twin daggers forged from cold iron, she hunts the beasts that lurk just beyond the edge of sight. No elusive phantom or burrowing nightmare can hide from her keen senses.

    Kaelith brings this relentless pursuit to the vast landscapes of code, serving as a master of Quality Assurance and Testing. She sets intricate traps in the form of unit tests, catching anomalies before they can breed into monstrous errors in production. Integration tests are her snares, ensuring that different components communicate without summoning chaotic side effects. To Kaelith, testing is not an afterthought. It is a necessary hunt to cleanse the repository of foul aberrations.
```

---

## Abilities

- Snare of the Unit: Lays a precise trap to catch and isolate individual logic errors before they escape.

- Integration Ambush: Ensures that intersecting pathways of code do not collide and spawn monstrous exceptions.

- Vision of the Edge Case: Pierces the veil of the unknown to anticipate and guard against rare and deadly user behaviors.

- Mocking Illusion: Summons phantoms of external services to test the system's defenses without risking real resources.

- Strike of Regression: A swift attack that verifies old wounds have not reopened, keeping past bugs securely in their graves.

- Poison of the Mutation: Injects deliberate faults into the codebase to test the strength of existing wards and defenses.
