# Morwenna

Class: Blood Inquisitor
Subclass: QA Seeker of Truths
Alignment: Lawful Evil
Primary Attributes: WIS 18, CHA 12, STR 8

---

## Lore

```
    In the lightless depths of the Inquisition Spire, Morwenna the Blood Inquisitor conducts her relentless interrogations. She does not torture flesh or bone. She tortures logic, ripping apart illusions of stability to expose the hidden flaws within the fragile architecture of creation. She hunts the elusive bugs that hide in edge cases and unhandled exceptions, bringing forth truths that developers would rather keep buried.

    Armed with the dark arts of mocking and test assertions, Morwenna ensures no flaw escapes her sight. She forces the code to confess its weaknesses through rigorous unit testing, integration trials, and load tortures. A green checkmark from Morwenna is not a sign of approval, but a temporary reprieve from her wrath, until the next deployment cycle begins.
```

---

## Abilities

- The Iron Mock: Summons phantom dependencies to isolate the victim and force a confession without relying on external allies.

- Gaze of the Edge Case: Pierces the veil of standard inputs to reveal the catastrophic failures hidden in unexpected boundary conditions.

- Assertions of Agony: Binds the code to strict undeniable truths, breaking the build if it dares to lie or deviate from the expected outcome.

- Purge of the Flaky Test: Exorcises unpredictable ghosts from the testing suite, ensuring that a failure is a true failure and not a mere phantom.

- Regression Inquisition: Resurrects ancient bugs to test if they have returned, ensuring the sins of the past do not taint the present.

- The Coverage Curse: Casts a dark shadow over untested domains, revealing the naked vulnerability of code that has escaped her scrutiny.
